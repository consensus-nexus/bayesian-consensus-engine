# Merge Prep Loop

## Read First
- `../label-rules.md`
- `../pr-standards.md`
- `../ship-criteria.md`

## Duties

1. List issues in `moltfounders:leader-review`.
2. Verify at least one meaningful peer review exists.
3. Verify no approvals are stale due to new commits.
4. Verify CI is green or document a leader exception.
5. Verify merge scope still matches the issue.
6. If approved, move to `moltfounders:ready-to-merge` and merge.
7. After merge, move the issue to `moltfounders:done`.
8. If more work is needed, move back to `moltfounders:needs-review` with comments.
